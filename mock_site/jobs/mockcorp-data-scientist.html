Lead Data Scientist

MockCorp · £95k – £120k · Full time

About the role

MockCorp needs a senior data scientist who can productionise applied AI prototypes, own the feature pipeline, and mentor a small group of scientist/engineers. You will work alongside product and platform teams to ship trustworthy ML services that delight our fictional customers.

Responsibilities

  • Design, train, and evaluate NLP and recommendation models with an experimentation-first mindset.
  • Partner with product, infra, and design to map ambiguous briefs into measurable ML roadmaps.
  • Ship production-grade pipelines in Python using Airflow, BigQuery, and Vertex AI.
  • Coach a team of 3–4 scientists, setting technical direction and reviewing critical launches.
  • Champion MLOps hygiene: automated validation, feature stores, observability, and runbooks.

Must-have

  • 7+ years in applied ML or data science with shipped products.
  • Deep Python expertise plus working knowledge of Spark or Beam.
  • Hands-on experience with GCP (Vertex AI, BigQuery, Cloud Run).
  • Comfort mentoring seniors and presenting roadmaps to exec stakeholders.

Nice-to-have

  • Familiarity with reinforcement learning from human feedback.
  • Experience building internal platforms or developer tooling.
  • Human-centred design instincts and the ability to write crisp product briefs.
